Ridgeview Village Condominiums HOA

Our Home

Ridgeview Village is a small 44 unit gated community just east of the Smoketree neighborhood at the intersection of El Cielo and Escoba in Palm Springs. This location is shelterd by and has majestic views of Mt. San Jacinto mountains to the south and west. Amenities include two community pools, spas and tennis courts. The common areas have mature trees and landscaping. Each unit has a two-car garage. Ridgeview Village is conveniently located minutes from the Palm Springs airport, City Hall and county services and within walking distance of the Smoketree shopping complex.
